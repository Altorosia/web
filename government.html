Government Structure

Altorosia operates as a republican democracy, governed by elected or appointed leaders chosen by the people. The structure ensures transparency, accountability, and active civic participation in national decisions.

Our government is built on transparency, citizen participation, and civic responsibility. Every voice matters, and every citizen has the opportunity to lead, serve, or influence decision-making.
